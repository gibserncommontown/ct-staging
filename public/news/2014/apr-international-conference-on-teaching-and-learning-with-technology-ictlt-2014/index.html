Apr: International Conference on Teaching and Learning with Technology (iCTLT) 2014


We participated in the International Conference on Teaching and Learning with Technology was held on 07-10 April 2014 at Suntec Singapore Convention & Exhibition Centre. It was a premier educational technology conference where the leaders connect to chart the future of learning.
One of the many products that we showcased at the event was CommonAcademy. It is a Learning Content Management System (LCMS) which connects students and teachers virtually. The main features are primary management of learning content, performance reporting of training results, event scheduling, etc.
